The Smart Syringe:
Adaptive Magnetorheological Fluid

Presenter Memory Anchors (Yassin):
Syringe Analogy: Traditional landing gear is like a regular syringe filled with normal oil. It remains stiff and unadaptive.
Millisecond Control: Microscopic iron particles in synthetic oil align instantly under an electromagnet, turning fluid thicker to adjust structural stiffness.
Weight Balancing: Thicken fluid instantly for heavy landings, thin out fluid for light landings. Safe and incredibly smooth touchdowns.
